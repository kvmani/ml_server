What is Microstructure Super-Resolution?

Microstructure Super-Resolution refers to enhancing the resolution and clarity of low-quality microstructural images using advanced deep learning techniques.

How does it work?

Our system uses state-of-the-art Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) to transform low-resolution inputs into detailed, high-resolution images. These models are trained on high-quality data to realistically reconstruct missing details and refine image quality in a data-driven manner.

What is EBSD Clean-Up?

EBSD Clean-Up is a utility to post-process Electron Backscatter Diffraction (EBSD) data, enhancing orientation maps by reducing noise, correcting artifacts, and improving grain boundary visualization.

What file formats are supported?

The tool supports standard EBSD file formats, including .ang, .ctf, .osc, .h5, and related formats.

How do I get started?

Navigate to the home page, choose your desired tool (Super-Resolution or EBSD Clean-Up), and follow the instructions to upload and process your data.

What are the system requirements?

All tools are fully web-based and require only a modern browser such as Chrome, Firefox, or Edge. No additional software installation is necessary.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We ensure that all uploads are handled securely. Your data is not permanently stored and is only used for temporary processing during the session.
